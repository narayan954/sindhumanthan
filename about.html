Welcome To Sindhu Manthan Foundations


Samvedna Foundations is an Non-governmental Organization (NGO) founded on April 16th, 2022 with the aim of promoting sparrow conservation and providing education to underprivileged children. 

The organization operates two main initiatives, the first being "Ashiyana," which raises awareness about the importance of sparrows and promotes sparrow conservation efforts. The second initiative is called "Samvedna Gyanshala," and it provides basic education and support to children in slum areas who lack access to educational resources.

About Our Charity

Sindhu Manthan Foundation envisions a society where everyone lives with dignity, respect, and equality.

To achieve this we focus on promoting community welfare for the most marginalized and underprivileged sections of society. We work on issues and concerns that most affect the marginalized populations in Noida. As a public interest civil society organization and local advocacy group, Manthan helps to create spaces in the community, in key institutions, and in public policies for dialogue and engagement on issues related to social development. The overarching goal of the organization is to promote community welfare for most marginalized populations with a particular emphasis on women empowerment, child development, healthcare and education, and a sustainable environment.
